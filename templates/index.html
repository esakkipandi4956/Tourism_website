House Boating

Our family houseboat trip through the serene backwaters was a magical experience, gliding along tranquil canals, surrounded by lush greenery and local village life, where we relished delicious home-cooked meals on the deck as the sun set, casting golden hues on the water, while the kids excitedly spotted birds and small water creatures, creating memories that felt like a floating paradise; it was a perfect blend of relaxation and exploration, leaving us completely rejuvenated.

The Kerala backwaters are ideal for a family visit at any time of the year. If you want to avoid the monsoons, plan your trip between August and May.
